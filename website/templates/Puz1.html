transported back in time to the Cave Man Era. As they venture deeper into this primitive

Rules of Wordle (Cave Man Era Edition):

1. You have 5 attempts to guess the secret five-letter word.
2. Each attempt is represented by a word of your own, where:
- A letter turns green if it's in the correct position.
- A letter turns yellow if it's in the secret word but in the wrong position.
- A letter remains white if it's not in the secret word.
3. Use these clues to deduce the secret word and solve the puzzle.
4. Your task is to decipher the ancient word with logic and wit,
but remember, you only have 5 chances to prove your word mastery.
5. Can you crack the code and unlock the secrets of the Cave Man Era?
